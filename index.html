What is Goloi?

Goloi is a digital platform that connects car service centers and potential customers. Using our expertise , car service centers can increase their visibility online and reach new clients through our website and social media pages.

Why use Goloi?

The platform offers car service centers the following:

  • Online marketing to reach new customers
  • Schedule and manage your booking online for yours and your customer convenience
  • Safe online payments
  • Professional invoices that are sent to your customers automatically
  • Monthly bookings and income summary

How it works?

Log on to your system by phone or computer:

  • Select days and slots where there is availability
  • Receive booking requests based on your availability
  • Send quotation to the client
  • Receive booking confirmation
