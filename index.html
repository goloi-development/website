What is Goloi?

Goloi is a mobile App which car owners can use to find car service centers in their area,
see and compare base prices and make a booking online in minutes.
You do not need to make a call or send an email, Goloi connects you with your preferred service center, with a few touches on your phones.

With Goloi we help you decide:
Where
When
Who will service your car without losing your vehicle warranty.
